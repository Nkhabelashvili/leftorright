Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the Photo Rating App collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the website.

1. Consent

By using this website and checking the agreement box during registration, you consent to your photo being saved in our database and rated by other users in comparison to other people's photos.

2. Data Collection and Use

We collect and store the following information:

  • Your NYU email address
  • Your uploaded profile picture
  • Rating data generated from user interactions

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Disclaimer

This website is not associated with or endorsed by New York University (NYU) in any official capacity. It is an independent project created for entertainment purposes only.

5. Purpose

This website is intended for entertainment purposes only and should not be taken seriously. The ratings and comparisons made on this platform are subjective and do not reflect any official evaluation or assessment.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We encourage users to frequently check this page for any changes.
